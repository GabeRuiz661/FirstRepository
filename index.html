Bee Movie is the best film of all time. Bee Movie is about Barry B Benson, a bee who got his species to stop working, but it takes a turn for the worst. He soon realizes that bees work for free, to keep life running.

Major Characaters

Barry B Benson is like every other bee in the hive, except he wants to stop working for the rest of his life. He tries out for the pollen jocks, only to eventually meet Vanessa after a tennis incident.

Adam is Barry's friend. He wants to try out for the krelmin, and tries to get his friend to start "Thinking Bee".

Vanessa Bloom is Barry's love interest and Ken's ex girlfriend. Her parents wanted her to be a doctor or a judge, but she wanted to be a flower enthusiast.
